Privacy Policy for Steps Count

Last updated: 2026-02-17

Steps Count ("the app") is designed to be privacy-first. This policy explains what data is accessed, how it is used, and what choices you have.

If you have questions, contact: app@parfocalis.com

1) Summary

  • We do not require account creation.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.
  • We do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.
  • Health and usage data used by the app is intended to stay on your device.

2) Data We Access

The app can request permission to read:

  • Step Count from Apple Health (HealthKit)

The app accesses this data only to provide core app features:

  • today's steps
  • progress toward your goal
  • 7-day trends and averages
  • optional Live Activity display

If you deny Health access, some features will not work.

3) Data Storage and Processing

  • App data is only stored locally on your device.
  • No cloud account is needed for core functionality.

4) Data Sharing

We do not collect, have access to, or share your personal health data. Your data is not shared with third parties for advertising or tracking.

We may rely on Apple platform services needed for app distribution and operation (for example App Store and TestFlight infrastructure), which are governed by Apple policies.

5) Analytics and Aggregated Information

The app itself is designed to operate without personal tracking.

Separately, we may view aggregated, non-personal metrics provided by Apple (for example App Store Connect reports such as downloads, retention, and crash trends) to improve product quality.

These metrics do not provide us with your raw HealthKit records.

If we add any additional analytics tooling in the future, we will:

  • update this policy before release
  • update App Store privacy disclosures accordingly

6) Live Activities and Notifications

If you enable Live Activity, the app may display your current progress on supported system surfaces (such as Lock Screen/Dynamic Island), based on your device settings and iOS behavior.

You can disable this feature in the app at any time.

7) Your Choices and Controls

You can control data access and app behavior by:

  • Granting or revoking Health access in iOS Settings
  • Enabling or disabling Live Activity in the app
  • Uninstalling the app (which removes app-local data, subject to iOS backup behavior)

Health permissions can be managed at:

  • iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Steps Count

8) Data Retention

Because app data is intended to be stored on-device, retention generally follows your use of the app and your device lifecycle.

If you delete the app, local app data is removed from the device, subject to standard iOS backup/restore mechanisms.

9) Children's Privacy

Steps Count is a general audience app and is not directed to children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10) International Users

If you use the app outside your home country, app distribution and related services may involve Apple infrastructure in other regions, subject to Apple's terms and privacy practices.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above and publish the revised version at this URL.

12) Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact:

  • Email: app@parfocalis.com
  • Support page: https://parfocalis.com/stepscount
