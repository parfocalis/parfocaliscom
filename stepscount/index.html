Steps Count — Support

Thank you for using Steps Count. This page explains how to get help.

Contact

For questions, feedback, or technical support:

  • Email: appsupport@parfocalis.com

We aim to respond within a few business days.

Before You Contact Us

  • Step count not updating? Make sure you've granted HealthKit access to Steps Count in Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices.
  • Widget or Live Activity not showing? Check that the app has permission to show data and that your device supports the feature.
  • Privacy or data: See our Privacy Policy.

App Info

  • App name: Steps Count
  • Platform: iOS (iPhone)
  • No account required — your data stays on your device.
